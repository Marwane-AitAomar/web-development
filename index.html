Our Story

Cooked by chefs,
not algorithms.

We believe great food shouldn't live only in fine dining rooms.

Foodie was founded in 2018 by Chef Marco Bellini and sommelier Priya Nair — two people who met at a three-Michelin-star kitchen and left to build something more accessible. Every dish on our menu is designed, tested, and refined in our open kitchen in the city centre.

We source produce from a rotating roster of local farms within 100 miles, change our seasonal specials every 10 days, and never freeze anything. Our packaging is 100% compostable and our delivery fleet runs on electric vehicles.
